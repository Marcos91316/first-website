Argentina Cuisine

My parents have made these foods when I was little and I want to share the experience on how good they are.

My top three favorite food

  • Empanadas
  • Asados
  • Flan

If you click this picture, you will find a recipe for empanadas ;)

What makes an argentinean empanada different from other countries is that the dough is flour based instead of corn. They can be filled with beef, chicken, ham and cheese, sweet corn or swiss chard and cheese before being sealed or turned over.

If you click this picture, you will find a recipe argentinian asado ;)

An asado is meat grilled to its purest form, traditionally the fire used to cook the meat is made with a combination of red-hot coal and firewood, though the exact type of wood may vary from region to region.

Flan is one of my favorite argentinean desserts, its like a custard. Click the picture to find out how to make it

The flan mixto, also called Flan casero is a classic of Argentina but also Uruguayan and Mexican tables, and is none other than a flan served with ducle de leche and whipped cream.
